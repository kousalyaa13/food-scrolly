CALORIES vs FAT

More calories usually mean more fat.
But not every food plays by the same rules.

🔍 Calories and fat are related, but the type of food matters a lot. Two foods can have similar calories but very different fat levels depending on their category. Hover the dots to explore every food!

WHAT YOU'RE SEEING 🔍

  • 🍎 Fruits & Vegetables are generally the lowest in both calories and fat. → They cluster near the bottom-left, meaning they are lighter and lower in fat.
  • 🍟 Snacks tend to be much higher in both calories and fat. → Many points appear in the upper-right, showing they are more energy-dense.
  • 🥛 Dairy & Grains fall somewhere in the middle. → They have moderate calories and fat compared to other groups.
  • 🍗 Protein foods vary more than others. → Some are higher in fat, while others stay lower, so they are more spread out.
  • ··· Other foods show the widest range. → Some are extremely high in both, acting as outliers.
